Our Story

ONE DAY AT THE MALL, I WAS TOTALLY DRAWN IN BY THE SMELL OF FRESH BREAD. I STOPPED BY THIS LITTLE BAKERY AND PICKED UP A BAGUETTE IT WAS SIMPLE, COMFORTING, AND SO DELICIOUS THAT I COULDN’T STOP SMILING. THAT’S WHEN IT HIT ME “WHY NOT CREATE MY OWN PLACE WHERE EVERYONE CAN ENJOY THIS VIBE?”

AND THAT’S HOW FRENCH TOAST BAKERY GOT STARTED NOW WE’RE ALL ABOUT SERVING UP SWEET MOMENTS WITH OUR DELICIOUS BAKED GOODS AND FRENCH BREAKFAST. WHETHER YOU’RE POPPING IN FOR A QUICK SNACK, HANGING OUT WITH FRIENDS, OR ORDERING ONLINE WHILE CHILLING AT HOME, WE’RE HERE TO MAKE YOUR DAY A BIT BRIGHTER.

ONE SLICE AT A TIME.
